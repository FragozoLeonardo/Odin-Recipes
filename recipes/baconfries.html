Bacon fries with cheese

Ingredient list

  • 400 grams of frozen fries
  • 100 grams of cheese
  • 200 grams of bacon

Step by Step Cooking

  1. Fry the fries as usual.
  2. In a pan with oil, fry the bacon cubes until crispy.
  3. Melt the cheese in a refractory in the microwave.
  4. Mix the bacon and cheese.
  5. Place on top of already fried fries.
  6. Bake in low temperature oven for 5 minutes.
